My name is Nilam

An artist, designer, craftsman, researcher, or whatever you wanna call it

I was born in my hometown, Jakarta, Indonesia, in the summer of 1994. Shortly after, my family moved to Tokyo, Japan, where I fell in love with robots.

I grew up in many places around the world—Japan, Indonesia, Austria, and Singapore. I got my Bachelor's of Fine Art at Maryland Institute College of Art, Baltimore, MD, The US and pursued a Master's of Fine Art in Toronto, ON, Canada. Currently stationed in Jakarta for a break and looking for short term work and residency opportunity.

My goal is to never stop learning, never stop questioning.

With home in my suitcase, dreams in my pocket, the world is my oyster, so call me wherever you need me and I'll be there.
